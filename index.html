About Me

Hi! I'm John Rey, a passionate graphic designer and animator from Naga City. With a love for visual storytelling, I specialize in creating engaging designs that breathe life into ideas. My journey in the world of design began at a young age, and I’ve honed my skills in various styles, from sleek and modern to vibrant and whimsical. I thrive on collaboration and believe that the best results come from understanding my clients' vision. Whether I’m crafting a brand identity or bringing a concept to life through animation, I’m committed to delivering high-quality work that captivates and inspires. When I’m not designing, you can find me exploring local art, diving into new software, or enjoying the beautiful landscapes of my hometown. Let’s connect and create something amazing together!
